About

I'm Fabio Garzotto – a Customer Experience and Operations leader helping companies scale without losing what makes them human.

Over 15+ years, I've built operations from scratch at startups and scaled teams of 100+ at companies like roadsurfer, CHECK24, PULPO WMS, and Build.One. I've learned that great CX isn't just about vision – it's about the processes, systems, and operations that deliver it consistently at scale.

Now, as we enter the AI era, that lesson matters more than ever. AI can transform customer experience and operational efficiency – but only if we build the right foundations. Too many companies deploy AI that breaks their brand promise, automates the wrong things, or scales inefficiency instead of excellence.

My focus: Building scalable, customer-centric operations where AI amplifies human connection rather than replaces it. Where automation serves both efficiency and loyalty. Where the invisible machinery of great operations creates effortless customer experiences.

This means getting the fundamentals right: processes that scale, teams that execute, systems that integrate, and AI that enhances rather than commodifies. It's the intersection of strategic vision and operational reality – because ideas without execution are just expensive slides.

Background: Director CX at roadsurfer. Former COO at PULPO WMS and Build.One. Wirtschaftsingenieur from KIT. Based in Munich/Starnberg.
